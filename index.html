Frequently Asked Questions

When will EventPass launch in Port Harcourt?

We're planning to launch in Q3 2025. Waitlist members will get early access to our beta version.

Is it free to use?

Yes, EventPass will be free for event attendees. Event hosts will have free basic listings with premium options available.

How can I become an event host?

Select "Hosting Events" when signing up for the waitlist, and we'll provide you with early access to our host tools.

Will you expand to other cities?

We're starting with Port Harcourt to build a strong local community. Expansion plans will be announced after our initial launch.
